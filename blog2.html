Top 5 Bug Bounty platforms for 2021

14th July, 2021

Once a valid vulnerability has been reported by a security expert, the organization assesses it and pays the expert a fee. That's how it works.

HackerOne -

HackerOne is without a doubt the world's largest ethical hacking community. Experts from almost all countries participate and collaborate on this platform. They host their bug bounty code on the largest companies. Hackers have earned more than $ 100 million in financial compensation for vulnerabilities and vulnerabilities in web applications.
Learn more...

BugCrowd -

BugCrowd is a similar platform that allows you to participate as a security researcher and help companies find security loopholes on their sites. They offer many financial services. You can also join to make money. Bugcrowd is as big as Hackerone and has programs from top companies like Tesla, Netflix, etc.
Learn more...

Intigriti -

Intigriti is one of the largest online communities of cyber security experts in Europe. It is relatively new but is growing very fast. Intigriti is focused on Europe and some of the great shows you will find are Red Bull, Tomorrowland, Torfs and others.
Learn more...

SynAck -

SynAck is a well-known global penetration testing platform that works with clients around the world. As a security expert, you can join their "red team", an elite group of researchers from more than 80 countries. They have a detailed selection process, after which you will be admitted to a team of recognized experts. As a member, you work with some of the biggest brands to protect their systems and web applications.
Learn more...

Immunefi -

Immunefi is a state-of-the-art bug bounty platform for smart contracts and DeFi projects, where market researchers scan code, detect vulnerabilities, receive rewards and make cryptocurrency more secure.
