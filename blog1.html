Top 5 must know Defi platforms

04th August, 2021

What is DeFi?

Abbreviated from decentralized financing, DeFi is the overarching concept for various public space applications and projects in blockchain that aim to disrupt the traditional financial world.

Uniswap -

Uniswap is a decentralized sector that will dominate the DeFi network. The UNI token was launched in September 2020 by the UNISVAP protocol. It is one of the leading products in the industry with a growth rate of up to 1100% in the same range.

Founder: Hayden Adams
Market Cap: $13 Billion

Aave -

Aave was founded by Stani Kulechov. Aave is an open source and custodian-free protocol that allows users to earn interest on deposits and borrow cryptocurrency funds.

Founder: Stani Kulechov
Market Cap: $5 Billion

ChainLink -

ChainLink as a link between a huge amount of information that is exchanged between cryptographic applications. It is used to send real-time data to smart contracts located in a blockchain.

Founder: Sergey Nazarov
Market Cap: $7 Billion

Compound -

Compound is an Internet-based loan protocol that focuses on providing decentralized lending to cryptography users. Lenders and borrowers automatically connect through the Ethereum-based protocol.

Founder: Robert Leshner
Market Cap: +$3 Billion

Curve -

Curve is one of Ethereum's decentralized currency liquidity funds, specially tailored for highly efficient stable trading of bitcoins.

Founder: Michael Egorov
Market Cap: $4 Billion
